# MFA Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines when Multi-Factor Authentication should be required to protect user accounts and sensitive applications.

## Scope

This policy applies to employees, contractors, privileged users, and any user accessing business applications.

## MFA Requirements

MFA should be required for:

- Microsoft 365 access
- VPN or remote access
- Admin portals
- Finance and HR applications
- Privileged accounts
- High-risk sign-ins
- Access from unmanaged devices

## Stronger MFA Enforcement

Privileged users should be required to complete MFA every time they access admin-level applications.

## Exceptions

MFA exceptions should be limited, documented, approved by security, and reviewed regularly.

## Security Value

MFA reduces the risk of account compromise caused by stolen or weak passwords.
